Sliding Tiles

Created by Lemon & Lime Gamess

How to Play

Rearrange the numbered tiles in ascending order (1-8, with the empty space in the last position).

Click on a tile adjacent (horizontally or vertically) to the empty space to move it.

Try to solve the puzzle in the fastest time possible!
