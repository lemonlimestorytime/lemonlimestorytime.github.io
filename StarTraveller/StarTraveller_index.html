How to Play:

You are the floating blue ball.
Press and hold the UP Arrow or 'W' key to move up.
Press and hold the DOWN Arrow or 'S' key to move down.
Dodge the red obstacles to earn points.
If you hit an obstacle, you lose 1 HP. Game Over when HP reaches 0.
