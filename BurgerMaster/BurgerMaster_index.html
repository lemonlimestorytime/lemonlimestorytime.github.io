How to Play Burger Master

Your goal is to build the correct burger according to the customer's order before time runs out!

  • Click on ingredients in the tray to add them to your burger.
  • Ingredients must be added in the exact order shown in the current order list.
  • Click "Serve Burger" when you believe your burger is complete and matches the order.
  • Correct burgers earn points, incorrect ones deduct points.
  • Work fast! You only have 60 seconds!
